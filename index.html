I'm deeply interested in cybersecurity, and its role in the tech industry. It is set to play a pivotal role as we move forward in the coming years. Getting my feet wet in cybersecurity has been an overwhelming experience to say the least. The diversity of the field is so exciting to me, it holds a potential lifetime's worth of learning. CULTURAL

Fashion has played an influential role in my upbringing and is the form of art I connect with the most. I try to stay up to date with what's trending, mostly in streetwear, but I have a strong appreciation for high fashion too. Some may argue my classification of fashion as art, but I believe that for many people it is one of the purest creative expressions of their personality, worn on a daily basis. The cohesiveness of an outfit is in its colours, textures, materials, and individual fits, just as a painting's is its subject, stroke style, surface, and colour choice.

I am also passionate about music as both a listener and a creator. I started producing electronic music early 2016 and had a small stint DJing around Brisbane with my best friend around the same time. I still spend time here and there playing around in Ableton, primarily just as a creative outlet. I'll listen to just about anything but just to give you an idea - while the list is endless - some of my biggest influencers are electronic artists like Gesaffelstein, Porter Robinson, Asdek, JUSTICE and Disclosure.
PERSONAL

Eventually when I was around 9 or 10, I was given my first computer. I quickly found myself within the competitive gaming scene. I played Counter-Strike at a high level until other things took a higher priority in my life. I still spend a large portion of my spare time playing games recreationally.
